About ARC

Automotive Research Centre, or ARC, is an offshoot of the School of Mechanical Engineering that was established in 2004 under the Fund for Improvement of Science and Technology Infrastructure (FIST) with a funding of Rs 5,600,000 from the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India. The center was supported by the School of Mechanical Engineering until 2016, after which it was separated from the school to strengthen the infrastructure and research that relate to the future direction of the global automotive industry. More recently, VIT has invested Rs 5 crore towards the centre’s resources to assist the development of state-of-the-art technology in autonomous vehicles. State University of New York, Binghamton has also collaborated with ARC in VIT to promote the research further. Over the years, the centre has evolved into a place for the next generation of researchers and developers to nurture their ideas and polish their skills. It is providing a platform for them to publish their ideas and bring them to life so as to spread awareness about this technology among the masses and make an impact in the real world.

About IET On Campus

IET On Campus is a community that helps like-minded students get ahead and get noticed. We support students like you to build a community as a society or student organisation within your University by providing resources and funding for extracurricular events. It’s available to everyone studying engineering and technology related courses, accessible at your university and supported by your peers. Through the joint forces of the IET and your student population, On Campus gives you the resources, connections and inspiration you need to launch yourself into your career with confidence. It’s also an opportunity to pass your enthusiasm on to others and help us to develop more people’s futures on campus.
